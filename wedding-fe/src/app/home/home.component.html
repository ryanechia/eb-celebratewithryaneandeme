Our Story

Ryane and Emelyne met in 2005 in the first year of secondary school. With piercing eyes, melancholic stature, a good sense of humour and a great face, Ryane was a fun person to be with. He found Emelyne to complement him with a bubbly yet carefree nature, punny with an even prettier face and just as loud. He eventually left for Australia in 2006 with his family and maintaining contact was only by the means of an MSN chat platform or costly IDD calling cards.

However, it wasn’t until the Christmas of 2008 that locked in their persistence to maintain a long-distance relationship until Ryane decided to return home (to Emelyne) and serve the nation in 2010.

As luck would have it, they both were able to further their university education in Australia and the rest, as they say, is history.

They’re so excited to finally start the next phase of their lives together and can’t wait to celebrate with you to happen.
